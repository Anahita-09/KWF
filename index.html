Hey there! 🫶

Kode With Friends (K.W.F) is a program that introduces middle and high school students to the fundamentals of coding. Participants will get to create their projects through mentorships, interactive lessons, and workshops.

Participants will explore popular programming languages like Python, HTML, JavaScript, Java, SQL, and Snap Berkeley. This program offers a supportive environment for Participants who aspire to be software developers or are interested in coding and building a strong foundation for future success in the STEM field.

Meet Our Mascot: KWF 🤖

KWF is a friendly robot from the planet Algorithma, where everyone enjoys coding; everyone on the planet codes their dreams into reality. One day, KWF flies to Earth with a mission: print("Empower the next generation through coding!"). With his bright eyes and pixelated sparks, he begins sharing his knowledge, turning ideas into programs, and appearing on screens all around the world - teaching loops, fixing bugs, and encouraging every brave new coder!
